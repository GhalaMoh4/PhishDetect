About PhishDetect

PhishDetect is a machine learning-based tool that detects email phishing attempts using the Support Vector Machine (SVM) algorithm. The project aims to improve email security and prevent phishing attacks, which can lead to data breaches and financial losses. Developed by [Ghala,Taif,Ghaida,Leen] from King Khalid University, Department of Computer Science.

About PhishDetect

we used a dataset of real-world phishing emails to train an SVM algorithm to detect phishing attempts with high accuracy. We also experimented with different hyperparameter and feature engineering to optimize the model's performance. PhishDetect is an important tool for anyone looking to enhance their email security and protect themselves from phishing attacks.
